Synchronize hours displayed on website with hours published on Google Business Profile.

== Description ==

The Business Profile Hours Synch plugin allows you to easily display your Google Business hours on your website using a shortcode. 

= Features =
* Automatic updates from Google Business
* Mobile-responsive design
* Clean and professional layout

= Requirements =
* WordPress 5.0 or higher
* Divi Theme or Divi Builder Plugin
* PHP 7.4 or higher

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/divi-business-hours` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Use the shortcode [google_business_hours] to display the business hours on any page or post.

== Usage ==

1. Enter your Google Business ID in the module settings
2. Enter your Google Places API key. Business hours are cached for 12 hours to reduce API calls.
3. Use the shortcode [google_business_hours] to display the business hours on any page or post.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I find my Google Business ID? =
You can find your Google Business ID in your Google Business Profile dashboard or from your Google Maps listing URL.

= Can I customize the styling? =
Yes, use css for customizing colors, fonts, spacing, and more.
